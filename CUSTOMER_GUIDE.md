# Customer Guide: How to Use the E-Commerce System

Welcome to our e-commerce platform! This guide will walk you through how to use the system as a customer, from browsing products to completing your purchases.

## Getting Started

### 1. Accessing the Website
- Open your web browser and navigate to the website URL (typically `http://localhost:5173` for development)
- The website features a responsive design that works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

### 2. Creating an Account
- Click on "Register" in the navigation bar
- Fill in your details:
  - Full Name
  - Email Address
  - Password (minimum 6 characters)
  - Phone Number (optional)
- Click "Register" to create your account
- You'll receive a confirmation message and be redirected to the login page

### 3. Logging In
- Click "Login" in the navigation bar
- Enter your email and password
- Click "Login" to access your account
- Your login session will be maintained until you log out

## Browsing Products

### 4. Home Page
- The home page displays featured products and categories
- Use the navigation bar to access different sections
- Toggle between light and dark mode using the theme switcher

### 5. Products Page
- Click "Products" in the navigation bar
- Browse all available products
- Use the search bar to find specific products
- Filter products by category using the category dropdown
- Sort products by price, name, or rating

### 6. Product Details
- Click on any product card to view detailed information
- See product images, description, price, stock status, and ratings
- Read customer reviews and ratings

## Shopping Cart

### 7. Adding Items to Cart
- On any product card, click the green "Add to Cart" button
- Items are added to your cart instantly
- The cart icon in the navigation bar shows the number of items in your cart

### 8. Viewing Your Cart
- Click the cart icon in the navigation bar
- Review all items in your cart
- Adjust quantities using the + and - buttons
- Remove items by clicking the "Remove" button
- View subtotal, shipping (free), and total price

### 9. Cart Persistence
- Your cart contents are saved locally in your browser
- Items remain in your cart even if you close the browser
- Cart data is preserved across sessions

## Checkout Process

### 10. Starting Checkout
- From the cart page, click "Proceed to Checkout"
- You'll be prompted to log in if you're not already authenticated
- Ensure your cart is not empty before proceeding

### 11. Checkout Information
- Review your order summary
- Enter shipping information (if required)
- Select payment method (Telebirr is currently supported)

### 12. Payment Processing
- Click "Complete Order" to initiate payment
- You'll be redirected to the Telebirr payment gateway
- Follow the instructions to complete your payment
- Upon successful payment, you'll be redirected back to the success page

### 13. Order Confirmation
- After successful payment, you'll see a confirmation page
- Your order number and details will be displayed
- Check your email for order confirmation (if email service is configured)

## Account Management

### 14. Viewing Profile
- Click "Profile" in the navigation bar (requires login)
- View and edit your personal information
- Update your name, email, and phone number

### 15. Changing Password
- From your profile, click "Change Password"
- Enter your current password and new password
- Confirm the new password
- Click "Update Password" to save changes

### 16. Payment Methods
- Access "Payment Methods" from your profile
- View saved payment information (if any)
- Add or update payment methods

### 17. Order History
- Click "Orders" in the navigation bar
- View all your past orders
- See order status, dates, and details
- Track order progress

## Additional Features

### 18. Dark Mode
- Toggle between light and dark themes using the switch in the navigation bar
- Your theme preference is saved and persists across sessions

### 19. Responsive Design
- The website adapts to different screen sizes
- Use on desktop, tablet, or mobile devices
- All features are accessible on any device

### 20. Search Functionality
- Use the search bar in the navigation to find products by name
- Search results update in real-time as you type

### 21. Categories
- Browse products by category
- Click on category names to filter products
- Categories help you find products more easily

### 22. Product Ratings and Reviews
- View star ratings on product cards
- Read detailed reviews on product detail pages
- Ratings help you make informed purchasing decisions

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues:
- **Can't add to cart:** Ensure the product is in stock
- **Checkout fails:** Make sure you're logged in and cart isn't empty
- **Payment issues:** Check your internet connection and Telebirr account
- **Page not loading:** Clear browser cache or try a different browser

### Support:
- If you encounter any issues, try refreshing the page
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're using a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

## Security and Privacy

- All personal information is encrypted and securely stored
- Payments are processed through secure gateways
- Your data is never shared with third parties
- Use strong passwords and keep them confidential
